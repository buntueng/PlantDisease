Fix applied to proposed_model.py
================================

Problem
-------
MixStyle created torch.distributions.Beta concentration parameters with
`dtype=x.dtype`. During AMP training, x can be float16. Beta sampling then
fails in environments where the underlying Dirichlet sampler does not support
Half/BFloat16.

Fix
---
Sample Beta coefficients in torch.float32 on the same device, then cast the
sampled lambda tensor back to x.dtype before feature mixing.

Validation performed
--------------------
1. Python compilation for all main scripts.
2. MixStyle forward with float16, bfloat16, and float32 tensors.
3. Full ChiliLiteGFNet 224x224 forward and backward pass.
4. End-to-end one-epoch synthetic fit_and_evaluate smoke test.
